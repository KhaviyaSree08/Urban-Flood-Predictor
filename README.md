# 🌊 AI Urban Flood Predictor & Smart City Dashboard

---

## 📌 Project Description

The **AI Urban Flood Predictor & Smart City Dashboard** is an intelligent disaster management platform designed to **predict and monitor urban flooding in real time**.

Rapid urbanization and climate change have increased the frequency of sudden floods in cities, causing severe damage to infrastructure and transportation.

This system uses **machine learning, weather data, and geographic information** to predict flood risks and display them through an **interactive smart city dashboard**.

The platform provides:

- Early flood warnings
- Flood risk visualization
- Safe route recommendations

These features help **citizens and authorities respond quickly during flood situations**.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Urban flooding is a **major challenge faced by modern cities** due to:

- Heavy rainfall
- Poor drainage systems
- Climate change

Most existing systems **detect floods only after they occur**, which leaves little time for authorities and citizens to respond effectively.

The **lack of real-time monitoring** also makes it difficult to identify flood-prone areas quickly.

Therefore, there is a need for a **predictive flood monitoring system** that can:

- Forecast flood risks in advance
- Provide real-time updates
- Improve disaster response

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Develop an **AI-based system to predict flood risks**
- Provide **real-time flood monitoring**
- Display **flood risk zones on an interactive city map**
